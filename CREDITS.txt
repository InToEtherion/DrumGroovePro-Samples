==============================================================================
DESCRIPTION
==============================================================================

The Salamander Drumkit is a high-quality, professionally recorded acoustic 
drum sample library featuring:

- Complete drum kit with multiple velocity layers
- Round-robin samples for natural variation
- Overhead microphone perspective
- Clean, studio-quality recordings
- Multiple articulations per drum

Instruments included:
- Kick drums (2 variations)
- Snare drums (2 variations with snares on/off)
- Hi-hats (closed, semi-open, open, foot stomps)
- Toms (high, low)
- Cymbals (crashes, rides, chinas, splashes)
- Percussion (cowbell, bell chimes)

==============================================================================
RECORDING DETAILS
==============================================================================

Format: WAV (44.1kHz, 16-bit)
Microphone Setup: Overhead stereo pair
Velocity Layers: Multiple (P, MP, F, FF)
Round-Robin Samples: Up to 20 variations per articulation
Total Sample Count: ~400 WAV files
Total Size: ~500MB

TECHNICAL NOTES
==============================================================================

Sample Format: WAV (PCM)
Bit Depth: 16-bit
Sample Rate: 44,100 Hz
Channels: Mono (per sample)
Mapping Format: SFZ

The samples are organized with overhead microphone perspective (OH folder).
Each drum has multiple velocity layers and round-robin variations for 
realistic playback.

Velocity Layer Abbreviations:
- FF = Fortissimo (very loud, velocity 110-127)
- F = Forte (loud, velocity 85-109)
- MP = Mezzo-Piano (medium-soft, velocity 43-84)
- P = Piano (soft, velocity 1-42)
